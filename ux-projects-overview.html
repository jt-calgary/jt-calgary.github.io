Before starting a project

Research the client

I find it helpful to know who I’m going to work with by understanding their brand and their products, if any.

Research the industry

Understanding the industry allows me to see what potential problems and solutions currently exist.

Understand the client's vision

With some background knowledge, I look to see how the client’s vision for the project fits into the whole picture.

TL;DR

I research whatever I can to gain better insight into what the project is about & understand the problem better.
